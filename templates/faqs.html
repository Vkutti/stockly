Frequently asked questions

Straight answers about Stockly—what it does, how it works, and how to think about the results.

01

What is Stockly?

Stockly is a project I built to forecast stock prices for the next trading day. It’s a learning-focused tool, not a brokerage or advisory service.

02

How does it work?

Stockly uses an LSTM model in TensorFlow, trained on historical prices from Yahoo Finance. The model outputs a single next-day estimate from recent market data.

03

Is it accurate?

No model is 100% accurate—especially in markets. I’ve tried to make reasonable design choices, but real-world prediction needs far more data, infrastructure, and domain expertise than a student project can provide.

04

Can I trade my whole portfolio on this?

Please don’t. Don’t make financial decisions based on Stockly alone. Use it to explore ML and markets—not as a signal for real money.
